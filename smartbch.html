SmartBCH

What is smartBCH?
Smart Bitcoin Cash (smartBCH for short) is a sidechain for Bitcoin Cash (BCH). It is compatible with Ethereum's EVM and Web3 API, providing high throughput- 1 billion gas every 15 second- for DApps in a fast, secure, and decentralized manner.
How do I use smartBCH?
We recommend Metamask as interface. To add smartBCH network to Metamask, @pjman7 has wrote a tutorial on read.cash
Where can I get smartBCH?
You can swap your BCH to smartBCH through hop.cash
